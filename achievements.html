Academic Journal Reviewer

I contribute to the academic community by peer-reviewing submissions for various scientific journals in the fields of AI, Machine Learning, Engineering, and Healthcare. My role ensures the quality and rigor of published research.

Journals I have reviewed for:
  • [Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence]

    Focus: This journal provides an international forum for rapid publication of work describing the practical application of AI methods in all branches of engineering.
